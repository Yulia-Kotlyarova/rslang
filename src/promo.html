Description for interval rules
When determining the interval after which the card with the studied word will be shown again, the result of the word study, the user's grade of the word's difficulty, as well as the time elapsed since the last study of this word are taken into account. If a user makes a mistake while entering a word in the input field, this word will be shown to the user in 10 minutes. If the user entered the word correctly or clicked the 'Show Answer' button without entering the word in the input field, depending on his settings, he may be asked to estimate the difficulty of the word. The user can estimate the difficulty of the word by clicking on one of four buttons: 'Again', 'Difficult', 'Normal', 'Easy'. Pressing the 'Again' button is equivalent to making a mistake while learning the word, the word will be shown in 10 minutes. The simpler the word, the longer the time interval after which it will be shown to the user again. The interval is calculated by multiplying the time elapsed since the last repetition of a word by a factor corresponding to the user-defined difficulty of the word.
